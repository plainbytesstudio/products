Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2025

At PlainBytes Studio, we take your privacy seriously. Our "Privacy-by-Design" philosophy means that your data belongs to you, and only you. This policy outlines how we handle information across our website and our software applications.

1. The 100% Offline Guarantee

Our primary mission is to provide powerful tools that work entirely on your local machine.

  • Applications: PurePixel, PrivateLabelQR, and other tools in our suite process all data (images, text, links) locally.
  • No Cloud: We do not own, operate, or use any cloud servers to process your files. Your original data and processed results never leave your device.

2. Information Collection

  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account or provide an email address to use our software.
  • No Telemetry: Our applications do not contain hidden tracking code, analytics SDKs, or background telemetry. We do not monitor how you use the software.
  • Website Usage: Our official website is a static landing page. We do not use cookies to track your browsing habits or collect personal data.

3. Microsoft Store & Payments

Our software is distributed exclusively through the Microsoft Store.

  • Payments: All financial transactions, billing information, and In-App Purchases (IAP) are managed by Microsoft. PlainBytes Studio never sees or stores your credit card details.
  • Licensing: Upon startup, our apps may communicate with Microsoft Store servers via encrypted system calls to validate your license status. This is a system-level process managed by Windows.

4. Permissions

Our apps request the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • File System: Used only to read files you select and save the processed results to your designated folders.
  • Internet: Used solely for Microsoft Store license validation and checking for app updates.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your files on any remote servers, there is zero risk of your data being compromised through a breach of our infrastructure. The security of your local files is protected by your own computer's operating system.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website or software may contain links to external sites (such as GitHub or the Microsoft Store). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party platforms.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or service offerings. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please reach out via our GitHub repository: https://github.com/plainbytesstudio

Your privacy is our baseline, not an afterthought.
